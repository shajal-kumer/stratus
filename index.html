Welcome to Stratus Cars

From our purpose built showroom near Hartley Wintney, in Hook, Hampshire we pride ourselves in offering used cars for sale at very high standards. Our experienced and knowledgeable staff are on hand to answer any questions you may have and we offer a comfortable no pressure sales environment.

We offer parts & labour warranties on any car sold, with extended warranties available. Stratus also offer full workshop facilities with servicing, MOT’s and diagnostics available on all makes of car.

We have an extensive range of used hatchbacks, saloons, people carriers and 4 x 4’s. With up to 90 cars in stock at one time we are able to ensure we always have a car to satisfy any requirement. All cars offered for sale are presented to the highest standard, with pre sales checks, mileages guaranteed and HPI checks done.
